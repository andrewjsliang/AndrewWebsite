Mr.Stick Man Races for the Exit is a game that uses python. To begin with, you control the stick man by using the arrow keys. You jump by pressing the space bar. You can hop on the platforms. If you jump from a high place, you die. You can press the restart button on the button to play again. If you reach the door, you win. This is from a book tutorial.
